Strategic takeaway

At €150/t, carbon costs consume €1.58B or 6.7% of portfolio revenue. E-Mobility faces the steepest relative impact (8.4%), while Powertrain & Chassis carries the largest absolute burden (€627M — 40% of total portfolio carbon cost). Bearings & Industrial is most resilient at 5.5%. Every 1% reduction in portfolio emissions intensity saves approximately €15.8M annually.
